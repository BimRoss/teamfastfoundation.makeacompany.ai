Our Mission

Reintegration isn't a moment. It's a relationship.

We exist because the hardest part of service often comes after the discharge papers are signed. Team FAST Foundation stands in that gap.

Coming home from service can feel like the loneliest deployment. Structure disappears. Brotherhood fades. The skills that kept you alive overseas don't translate to a civilian commute, a marriage in transition, or a quiet weekend that won't quiet down.

Team FAST Foundation was built by veterans and families who've lived it. We pair recreational therapy, peer connection, and continuity of care to give veterans a real off-ramp from isolation and a real on-ramp back into community.

The relationship doesn't end when a program ends. We stay in it for the long haul.

Faith

Belief that every veteran's life is worth fighting for, including their own.

Action

Programs that get veterans outside, off screens, and into shared experience.

Service

Continuing the mission of serving each other long after the uniform comes off.

Truth

Honest conversation about PTSD, suicide, and the long road of recovery.

Recreational Therapy

Get outside. Get connected.

Adaptive outdoor experiences led by trained therapeutic staff. We use shared challenge in the wild as the doorway into healing — because for a lot of veterans, that doorway is easier to walk through than a clinical one.

About Us

Who we are, and what we stand for.

Team FAST Foundation was founded by veterans and family members who saw too many friends slip through the cracks between active duty and a civilian life that didn't know how to receive them.

We're a small, volunteer-driven 501(c)(3). That means the people running this thing are the same people you'll meet in our programs. No layers, no bureaucracy, no executives drawing oversized salaries off your donation.

Transparency is part of the trust we're asking for. Every dollar tracked, every program measured, every story honored. If you want to dig into our governance or financials, the links to the right will get you there.
