Our Mission

Reintegration isn't a moment. It's a relationship.

We exist because the hardest part of military service often comes after the discharge papers are signed. Team FAST Foundation stands in that gap.

Coming home from service can feel like the loneliest deployment. Structure disappears. Brotherhood fades. The skills that kept you alive overseas don't translate to a civilian commute, a marriage in transition, or a quiet weekend that won't quiet down.

Team FAST Foundation was built by veterans and families who've lived it. We pair recreational therapy, peer connection, and continuity of care to give veterans a real off-ramp from isolation and a real on-ramp back into community.

Our flagship program, Camp Victor, is the entry point. The relationship doesn't end when camp ends.

Flagship

Camp Victor

A multi-day immersion in recreational therapy, peer connection, and shared challenge. Veterans arrive carrying a load most civilians will never understand. They leave with people who do.

  • Adaptive outdoor activities led by trained therapeutic staff
  • Small groups, deep relationships, no scripts

  • No cost to veterans or their immediate family

About Us

Who we are, and what we stand for.

Team FAST Foundation was founded by veterans and family members who saw too many friends slip through the cracks between active duty and a civilian life that didn't know how to receive them.

We're a small, volunteer-driven 501(c)(3). That means the people running this thing are the same people you'll meet at Camp Victor. No layers, no bureaucracy, no executives drawing oversized salaries off your donation.

We believe transparency is part of the trust we're asking for. Every dollar tracked, every program measured, every story honored. If you want to dig into our governance or financials, the links to the right will get you there.
